Get Help Today

Many women who come to The Rose House may have been through previous treatment episodes that did not result in continued sobriety and health. Research shows that the longer women are in treatment for addiction, the greater their chances of long-term change. The Rose House program uses a multi-faceted approach to treatment that has been effective with women experiencing addiction and dual diagnoses – women whose lives have been impacted by many losses and negative repercussions due to their addiction and mental health symptoms.

The evidence-based methods we utilize include:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy
  • Dialectical Behavior Therapy
  • Gender Specific Treatment
  • Holistic Integrative Approach

Our Mission

Women get better at The Rose House. We believe that by engaging in high quality, gender specific, extended care addiction and mental health treatment, women can experience sustained sobriety and emotional healing. Our mission is to provide evidence-based, dual diagnosis integrative treatment delivered by a compassionate staff within a dynamic community of recovery.
